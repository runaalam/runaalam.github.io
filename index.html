About Me

I’m an iOS engineer with a deep love for learning and exploring. My curiosity fuels not only my work but also my adventures as I travel the world, soaking in the beauty of God’s creation. I have two wonderful kids who, despite their contrasting personalities—like Sheldon and Messy—share an unbreakable bond. When I’m not coding, reading up on the latest tech, or strengthening my spiritual connection through prayer, you’ll find me crafting or gardening. I love breathing new life into old items, turning them into something fresh and beautiful. My journey is a blend of technology, creativity, exploration, and faith, all of which shape who I am.

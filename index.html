About Me

I am a passionate iOS developer with over 5 years of experience. My enthusiasm for innovation has led me into the immersive world of VisionOS app development. My work is fueled by a commitment to technical excellence, continuous learning, and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in iOS development.

Beyond the keyboard, I’m a lifelong learner and explorer. My passion for discovery takes me on journeys around the world, where I find joy and reflection in the beauty of nature and deepen my spiritual connection. At home, I’m a mother to two incredible kids. I balance my tech life with creativity, whether through crafting, gardening, or transforming old items into something fresh and unique. My journey is a blend of technology, creativity, exploration, and faith, all of which shape who I am.
